List of Oscar nominees, including IMDB identifiers
Source: https://github.com/DLu/oscar_data/blob/main/oscars.csv

Ceremony - ceremony number
Year
Class - generic class of oscar
CanonicalCategory - normalized category
Category - category as named in the year
NomId - nominee identifier
Film - film title
FilmId	- film identifier
Name - Nominee name (sometimes, in the first years, the nominee was associated to more than one movie, or to none)
Nominees - Nominees names, short form (in some cases there aretwo or more names, that should be separated)
NomineeIds - nominees identifiers (idem)
Winner - indicates the winning nominee
Detail - complementary information
Note - complementary information
Citation - complementary information
MultifilmNomination - if two or more films were associated to the award
